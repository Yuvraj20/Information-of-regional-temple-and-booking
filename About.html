What Is Morse Test

morse test is a experiment which is used to calculate Brake power , Indicated power , frictional power , mechanical efficiency of a multi cylinder SI engine.

What is indicated power?

power developed inside engine due to combustion of fuel.

What is brake power?

This is the power actually delivered by the engine and is therefore the capacity of the engine.

What is frictional power?

The power loss due to friction between the moving parts are called as frictional power.
